About Us

The African Think Tank is a community-focused initiative for Africans and friends of Africa.

  • Our mission is to build a thriving, growth-focused community of audacious and innovative Africans in the diaspora.
  • We envision a world in which we are able to share ideas, collaborate, build trusting relationships and ultimately thrive as a people.

We firmly believe in the incredible talent, intelligence, corporate, business and entrepreneurial expertise Africans possess. Imagine the amazing things we can achieve as a people if we had an opportunity to meet, network and transform our thoughts and ideas to actualized initiatives.

Events

Our Events

The African Think Tank is a non-profit initiative which hosts free, monthly meetups for Africans and friends of Africa in major US cities. Regardless of your industry - professionals, entrepreneurs, investors, vendors, small and large business owners, etc., we invite you to join us for these live gatherings and experience the incredible power we can harness when we work together.
